Card Sorting: Original Content: Kid Loki

About the Website

As of late, I have been obsessing over the latest character in Marvel creation. Kid Loki. I say "latest" and "creation" not because Loki is a character newly invented- he has been around since 1965- rather, he has gone through a new transformation, not only in physical appearance, but in character!

I want to create a site to help readers new to the marvel universe but interested in Kid Loki, having heard of him through whatever other means, to find a place to jump in and begin learning about him! I want to give readers a chance for a list of what to read should they want to learn about this young Loki on their own, as well as give spoiler summaries to those who do not have access to the books but still wish to learn what happens. I also want to provide more than just official information, but provide users with fan made content such as images, gifs, fan fiction, and fan art!

I sorted through my layout as I would introducing Loki. I wanted to begin with outright information about him, about his character, about his abilities, as well as give a reading list of where to start reading and learning about Loki.

Then I provided the titles of the series in which he appears that are relevant to Kid Loki, where I wanted to summarize the events and Loki's meddling hands regarding the event.

Lastly, I wanted to provide some images, writings, and other fan works available to the reader to enjoy relating to Kid Loki. It will also help them find communities and others who love Kid Loki.
